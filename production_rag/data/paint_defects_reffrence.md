# Paint Job Defects Reference Guide

## 1. Scratch
**Description:**  
Thin or deep linear marks visible on the painted surface.

**Appearance:**  
- Straight or curved lines  
- Usually darker or lighter than surrounding paint  
- Can be shallow or deep

**Common Causes:**  
- Contact with sharp objects  
- Poor handling during transport  
- Contaminated conveyor belt

---

## 2. Paint Bubble
**Description:**  
Raised circular or oval blisters on the paint surface.

**Appearance:**  
- Small dome-shaped bumps  
- Often appear after drying

**Common Causes:**  
- Moisture trapped under paint  
- Improper surface preparation  
- High humidity during painting

---

## 3. Orange Peel
**Description:**  
Uneven texture resembling the skin of an orange.

**Appearance:**  
- Wavy or bumpy surface  
- Lack of smooth finish

**Common Causes:**  
- Incorrect spray pressure  
- High paint viscosity  
- Improper spray gun distance

---

## 4. Paint Drip
**Description:**  
Excess paint accumulation forming downward streaks.

**Appearance:**  
- Vertical or curved paint runs  
- Thicker paint lines

**Common Causes:**  
- Excess paint application  
- Low curing temperature  
- Improper spray angle
